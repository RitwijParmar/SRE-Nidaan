Welcome to SRE Nidaan.
This quick demo shows exactly how to use it during an incident.

Step one.
Start with incident context.
Fill the primary signal, customer impact, recent change context, and affected services.
You can also select a preset to speed things up.

Step two.
Build or edit the final incident brief, then click Analyze Incident.
The system runs grounded reasoning, not just a generic text response.

Step three.
Review the output in three parts:
root cause, intervention logic, and the causal graph.
Use the graph inspector to check upstream and downstream relations before acting.

Step four.
Move to safety approval.
If you want to proceed, provide a clear reason and authorize intervention.
This keeps action human-gated and auditable.

Step five.
Submit analyst feedback.
Mark useful or add corrections so future responses improve.

Under the hood, SRE Nidaan uses QLoRA SFT, reward modeling, and RLHF,
with an MCP-inspired tool layer for controlled telemetry calls.

That is the full flow:
describe, analyze, verify, authorize, and improve.
