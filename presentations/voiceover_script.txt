Hi, welcome to SRE Nidaan.
Let me walk you through the exact workflow an on-call engineer can follow during an incident.

First, click Check System to confirm runtime health.
Then pick a preset, for example Auth Retry Storm, so we can start from realistic context quickly.

Next, review the final incident brief.
Edit it in plain language with what changed, what users are seeing, and what systems look saturated.
Once the brief is clear, click Analyze Incident.

Now we wait for grounded analysis.
When the result is ready, open the Causal Graph Workspace and inspect how failures propagated across services.
This is where you validate upstream and downstream dependencies before taking action.

After that, move to Safety Approval.
Write why the intervention is safe, then authorize with a human decision gate.

Finally, submit analyst feedback.
Mark the result useful or add a correction so future incident responses keep improving.

That is the full loop in SRE Nidaan:
describe, analyze, verify, authorize, and improve.
